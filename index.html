What We Do

In one week, students will receive help with:

  • Writing Essays
  • Completing Applications
  • Obtaining Recommendations
  • Navigating Financial Aid
  • Understanding Collegiate Life

Paint the Picture

The college mentors can provide students a better idea of collegiate life than anyone else. We've been in their shoes only a few years ago and know what information they need in order to succeed in college.

Help with Essays

We can provide quality assistance with the best way to craft an essay for various applications. The course will prepare the students for creative thinking and meticulous writing.

Grow Their Voice

Each student will have the opportunity to receive personalized help. We know that everyone has different backgrounds and experiences, and we want the colleges to get to know them for who they are.

Get Them Ready

College is in many ways the greatest learning experience. Students will have chances to become more worldly, educated contributors to society. These students are the next world leaders. We know they will do amazing things.

Package Their Talents

Whether it's the Common App or a state school application, we want to package their talents in the most appealing way possible.

Build Their Confidence

We want to build students' confidence that they will do great, whether it is in the college applications or life beyond high school.
